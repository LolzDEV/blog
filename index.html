About me

Hello there! I'm Lorenzo Torres and I'm a 16 years old amateur programmer, I currently live in Italy and I'm really interested in motorbikes, tech in general and physics. I decided to create this website so that I can share my daily adventures in the world of tech (or maybe also something else) with anyone who has interest in the subject.

About the website

As you have probably noticed the website is not so visually appealing, that's a choice: modern web development requires lot of effort and the result is often not so good in terms of performance, indeed I choose to just use plain html+css and only few javascript is really needed (code highlight for example). Also, I want this to look good in text based browsers like links.
